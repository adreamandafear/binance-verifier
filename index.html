Welcome to my binance trade compressor. I saw a lot of posts on Reddit about people worried that Binance had screwed them over and they had missing funds from transactions that weren't really cancelled, so I wrote a simple parser that takes in the Excel Spreadsheet that you can export from Binance, and summarizes the transactions. I initially wrote this for my own use, but decided to share it in case others might find it useful.
  • On binance, click "Export Complete Order History"
  • Click past 3 months, then Export
  • Upload the file below
  • Check that the number beside each coin matches Deposits - withdrawals + Binance balance
